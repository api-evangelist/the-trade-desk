# The Trade Desk GraphQL API

The Trade Desk is a demand-side platform for programmatic advertising. Their API covers campaign management, ad groups, targeting segments, creatives, deal management, and reporting across display, video, audio, and CTV.

**Endpoint:** https://api.thetradedesk.com/v3/graphql
**Documentation:** https://partner.thetradedesk.com/v3/portal/api/doc/GqlApiCallsPlatform
